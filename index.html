The Situation

Sports organizations were handed new legal obligations and zero new infrastructure to execute them.

The House v. NCAA settlement took effect July 1, 2025. Every Division I institution that opted in — 82% of all D1 programs — is now legally required to manage direct athlete compensation, NIL compliance, and revenue sharing simultaneously.

Federal executive orders now tie athletic department compliance to university funding eligibility. The cost of inaction is no longer just competitive. It's existential.
