The Mission

Built for the 98%

Only a small percentage of athletes will compete professionally. But 100% deserve to be professionally prepared. Whether your next chapter is corporate, entrepreneurship, graduate school, coaching — or something you haven't discovered yet — AthletIQ helps you prepare before your final season. Because life after sports shouldn't begin after sports ends.

NIL income and portal offers are real — but they're short-term. Career readiness is what travels with you to every school, every deal, and every job after.

Trust / Founder

Why I Built AthletIQ

As a former student-athlete and HBCU graduate, I experienced firsthand how the lessons learned through sports don't always translate into career opportunities. Over the past decade, I've built my career at three of the world's leading technology companies while helping organizations leverage cloud and AI. Along the way, I continued mentoring athletes and saw the same challenge over and over: incredible talent, but no personalized way to translate that experience into career success. AthletIQ was built to bridge that gap — combining AI with continuous coaching to help every student-athlete build confidence, communicate their value, and prepare for life after sports.
Fred Underwood — Founder, AthletIQ
Former Student-Athlete · HBCU Graduate · Cloud & AI Solutions Architect · Sports Tech Builder · Doctor of Business Administration Researcher
